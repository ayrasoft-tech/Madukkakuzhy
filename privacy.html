At Madukkakuzhy Dental Care, your privacy and the security of your personal and medical information are of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and handle your data when you visit our website, use our appointment booking services, or visit our clinic.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible dental care, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, age, phone number, and email address.
  • Health and Medical Information: Reason for your visit, appointment history, and any medical information you choose to share with us during consultations.
  • Technical Data: Standard usage data when you visit our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information (managed via essential cookies).

2. How We Use Your Information

We strictly use your information for healthcare and operational purposes, including:

  • Scheduling, confirming, and managing your dental appointments.
  • Maintaining accurate dental records to provide tailored treatments.
  • Communicating with you regarding your treatment, follow-ups, or clinic updates.
  • Improving our website and clinic services based on patient feedback.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your appointment data is transmitted securely and stored in protected databases. Access to your personal and medical information is restricted to authorized dental professionals and administrative staff who require it to perform their duties.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental regulation to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You may also request corrections to any inaccurate data or ask for the deletion of your records, subject to legal requirements for retaining medical files.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
